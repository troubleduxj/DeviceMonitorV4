## 快速验证命令

### 查看最新后端日志

### 监控实时日志
```powershell

```

### 查看错误日志
```powershell
Get-Content app/logs/error.log -Tail 20
```

### 查看日志文件状态
```powershell
Get-Item app/logs/*.log | Select-Object Name, Length, LastWriteTime
```
